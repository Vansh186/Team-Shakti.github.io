ABOUT

VD Inc. was formed in 2019 when a bunch of passion driven youngsters and a vision driven mentor decided to ‘Hack the Stereotypes’. Vansh had an overwhelming experience in the field of Deep Learning and Mern Stack Development who he wanted to potray into a group of indivisuals. There is inadequate representation of youngsters in the field of cybersecurity and VD Inc. was the spark of change.

The beginning is always the hardest, and the team had quite a few struggles trying to establish themselves. Due to the efforts of the team and the mentors, the club flourished. Here at VD Inc., everyone strives their best to learn and teach others what we have learnt.

VD Inc. is characterised by its peer mentoring and the loving bond that teammates share with each other. Newbies are introduced into the field by the more experienced seniors. Overall, we are a group of motivated artists who would support and encourage each other while venturing into the cyber world.
